Adopt a pet from Bulgaria, save a life!

Welcome to PetSelect!

PetSelect is an online, searchable database of homeless dogs and cats, located in the streets or shelters in Bulgaria, who are in desperate need of homes. Nearly 100 animal shelters, public and private, and rescue organizations throughout the country post new pets for adoption every other day, so that pet lovers from any EU country, from the comfort of their personal computers, can search for a pet that best matches their desires and needs.

The site partners with rescue organizations all across Europe to help smooth the adoption process for all parties involved. It also includes a pet-care resource directory and a library of free pet-care articles to help new pet owners meet their pets' needs and to prevent abandoning of pets.

Want to list a pet from Bulgaria for adoption? No problem, just sign up with PetSelect as an organization here! Yes, even if you are an individual!
